About Us

The Architek is a widely diverse architectural company that offers services ranging from building plans to finished images and to even interior decor. It is a company that started in the early Nineteen Hundreds (1900's) and is now fully established in over 20 countries of the world with at least three branches in each country. Because of this we have available designs from different countries and most probably a style for any and every taste.
